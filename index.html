About Me

I am an associate professor in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISyE) at the University of Minnesota.

I did my PhD in the Management Science and Engineering department at Stanford University, under Prof. Ramesh Johari. My doctoral thesis was titled Dynamic Analysis of Markets.

My research interests include game theory, mechanism design, and applied probability. Mainly, I am interested in modelling markets and service systems using tools and concepts from game theory, applied probability, and stochastic modelling.
